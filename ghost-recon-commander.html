At Loot Drop we put all our knowledge and experience into our games to make the best possible entertainment, and this includes Ghost Recon Commander. This game was nominated in the 30th Annual 2012 Gamemaster Golden Joystick awards!  We continue to create core games like Ghost Recon Commander with our upcoming titles.

Lead an elite squad in Tom Clancy’s Ghost Recon Commander, a fast-paced, strategic combat experience from Ubisoft that challenges everything you know about Facebook games!

Control up to three elite special forces “Ghosts.” Select one of three different classes: the sturdy and armored “Assault”, the ranged “Recon”, or the accurate and efficient “Specialist”. Fight your way through a wide variety of missions to earn rewards that allow you to customize your weaponry and equipment, upgrade your soldier’s capabilities, and build your own high-tech base of operations.

Playing with friends earns you additional rewards: you can recruit your friends’ characters to help, or have them hire you as backup. Additionally, your achievements in Ghost Recon Commander unlock bonuses in other Ghost Recon games and vice versa.

Key Features

  • Game on the Go – Players have the ability to pick up where they left off in Tom Clancy’s Ghost Recon Commander; it doesn’t matter if they’ve been away from the game for a day or for an hour, time is not a constraint.
  • Customization – Ghosts can be upgraded and customized with state-of-the-art weaponry, armor and near-future gadgets.
  • Asynchronous Co-op – Players will be able to have friends join them on missions or offer themselves up as a mercenary for hire.
