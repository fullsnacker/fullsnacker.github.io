World's Oceans In Extreme Danger

John Romero
May 13, 2007

As you all know, I'm not much of an activist. I like making and playing games and being a happy consumer like you all. But sometimes you have to wake up and pay attention to what's happening in the world while we're all at home having fun playing World of Warcraft or Gears of War.

As reported on Project Censored, the world's oceans are in extreme danger (story #3) and there's very little being done to reverse the devastating warming trend that is changing everything about the planet's most vital resource.

If you can't get out there and do something directly at least you can do something in a minor way: vote for a president that will actually do something about this problem, for real. Bush is most definitely not that person, nor do I suspect, any Republican president unless I see an extreme environmental conservation strategy on the bill.

VOTE!
